# FAQ

## Q: What are your business hours?
A: We are open Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM local time. For more details see [our contact page](https://example.com/contact).

## Q: How can I reset my password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot password." Enter your email and we will send a reset link. The link expires in 24 hours. Learn more at [password help](https://example.com/help/password).

## Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfer. Invoicing is available for enterprise accounts.

## Q: How do I contact support?
A: You can reach support by email at support@example.com, or use the live chat on our website during business hours. [Support portal](https://example.com/support).

## Q: What is your refund policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for first-time subscribers. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days. See our [refund policy](https://example.com/refunds).

## Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: In your account settings, go to Billing > Subscription and click "Cancel subscription." Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

## Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes. New users get a 14-day free trial with full access. No credit card is required to start. [Start free trial](https://example.com/signup).

## Q: Do you offer API access?
A: Yes. API access is included on Pro and Enterprise plans. Documentation is at [docs.example.com](https://docs.example.com/api).

## Q: How is my data protected?
A: We use encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). We are SOC 2 Type II certified. Details: [security overview](https://example.com/security).

## Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes. From Account > Data you can export your data as CSV or JSON. Enterprise customers can request bulk exports via support.
