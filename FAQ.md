# FAQ

# A. About MAITRI and Its Role

## Q:What is MAITRI?	
A: MAITRI (Maharashtra Industry, Trade and Investment Facilitation Cell) is a dedicated cell for investment promotion in Maharashtra, providing integrated G2B services, investor support, grievance redressal, policy recommendations, and related facilitation.

## Q:What services does MAITRI offer?	
A: MAITRI facilitates approvals, provides handholding, resolves grievances, coordinates with departments, and acts as a central repository of investment-related information.

## Q:What is the MAITRI Portal?	
A: It is an online platform currently offering 128 services from 14 departments, enabling investors to apply for approvals, track applications, and access policies and incentives.

## Q:Does MAITRI support foreign investors or NRIs?	
A: Yes, MAITRI provides dedicated support to foreign investors and NRIs, including guidance on FDI policies, land availability, and regulatory compliance.

## Q:Can MAITRI help with district-level investments?	
A: Yes, MAITRI works in coordination with Regional Officers (ROs) and District Authorities (General Managers, District Investment Centre (GM-DICs) to promote local investment opportunities and facilitate regional approvals. 

# B. Legal Framework and Policy Support

## Q:What is the MAITRI Act, 2023?	
A: The Act provides a legal framework for the Single Window System, ensuring time-bound approvals, transparency, and accountability in investment facilitation.

## Q:What are the MAITRI Rules, 2025?	
A: Notified on 5th August 2025, these Rules operationalize the MAITRI Act by defining timelines, responsibilities, and escalation mechanisms for investor services.

## Q:What is the Empowered Committee under the MAITRI Act?	
A: It monitors service delivery timelines, addresses escalated grievances, and ensures accountability across departments.

# C. Approvals & Application Process

## Q:How can I identify the approvals required for my project?	
A: Use the Investment Wizard Module on the MAITRI Portal to identify applicable permissions based on your project type, location, and sector.

https://maitri.maharashtra.gov.in/wpcontent/portal/investor_run_wizard

## Q:Can I apply for multiple approvals through a single application?	
A: Yes, the MAITRI Portal allows bundled applications for multiple services across departments.

## Q:Can I track my application status online?	
A: Yes, real-time tracking is available under the “Services Applied” section of the MAITRI Portal.

## Q:What happens if my application is delayed?	
A: Delays beyond the notified timeline are escalated and decided by the Empowered Committee for further resolution.

## Q:Can I authorize a consultant or representative to apply on my behalf?	
A: Yes, authorized representatives can apply and manage services on behalf of investors through the MAITRI Portal.

# D. Investor Support & Grievance Redressal

## Q:How can I raise a grievance or seek help?	
A: You can email at maitri-mh@mah.gov.in or call the helpline at 1800 233 2033 (Toll Free)

## Q:Can I get help with billing errors, incentives, or local-level issues?	
A: Yes, you can contact MAITRI via email or helpline for assistance with any investment-related issue.

## Q:How does MAITRI ensure transparency in service delivery?	
A: Through real-time tracking, defined timelines, automated alerts, and escalation protocols.

## Q:Can I get post-approval support from MAITRI?	
A: Yes, MAITRI provides end-to-end support, including help with utilities, compliance, and grievance redressal.

# E. Sectoral & Strategic Facilitation

## Q:Are there sector-specific desks or facilitation cells?	
A: Yes, MAITRI works with sectoral departments and has dedicated desks for key sectors.

## Q:What qualifies as an Ultra Mega or Mega Project?	
A: Projects are classified based on investment size and employment potential. These categories receive special incentives and fast-track facilitation.
Ultra-Mega:
Minimum FCI: INR 4,000 Cr
Minimum Direct Employment: 4,000 persons

Mega Projects:
Area classification ranges from Zone A to Zone D+ including naxal areas.
Minimum FCI: 200 Cr to 1,500 Cr
Minimum Direct Employment: 350 to 2,000 persons

For more details, please refer PSI 2019 Scheme.

## Q:Is land allotment facilitated through MAITRI?	
A: MAITRI coordinates with MIDC and other agencies to assist investors in identifying and acquiring suitable land parcels.

# F.Digital & Technical Support

## Q:Is there a preferred browser for using the MAITRI Portal?	
A: Use updated versions of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge with a stable internet connection for best performance.

## Q:Are there any fees for using MAITRI services?	
A: All services on the MAITRI Portal are free. Please note that certain statutory fees may be applicable for specific approvals, as per the respective department’s norms.

# G. Events & Investment Promotion

## Q:What is MAITRI’s role in global events like Davos or Magnetic Maharashtra?	
A: MAITRI supports the Government in facilitating MOUs, coordinating with investors, and ensuring follow-up for project implementation.

## Q:How does MAITRI contribute to Ease of Doing Business in Maharashtra?	
A: By streamlining approvals, reducing timelines, enhancing transparency, and providing investor-centric support, MAITRI plays a key role in improving the business environment in the state.
